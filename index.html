Umesh Sharma

I am a graduate student at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Carnegie Mellon University. I am pursuing my graduate studies focusing on mechanics, data science, and computation. I am a Scott Institute Intern at The Wilton E. Scott Institute for Energy Innovation . Previously, I was part of BHISM Lab at the   Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati as a research assistant under the mentorship of  Dr. Hrishikesh Sharma where my research project focued on multiscale modeling of honeycomb structured composite materials and blast-induced brain traumatic injuries. I have extensively used numerical modeling and simulation techniques to explore some these problems from a solid mechanics perspective
