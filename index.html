R Umesh

I am a Junior Research Fellow at the  Department of Aerospace Engineering, Indian Insitute of Technology Bombay. I am working in the areas of atomistic simulations of materials, high performance scientific computing, and machine learning. I am specifically using machine learning driven molecular dynamic simulations to study the structures of amorphous materials. Previously, I was part of BHISM Lab as a research assistant under the mentorship of   Dr. Hrishikesh Sharma working on a project on impact mechanics.
